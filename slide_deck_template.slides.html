(Students Performance Exploration )¶

by (Basant Hussein)¶

Investigation Overview¶

The Inspiration is to understand the influence of the parents background, test preparation etc on students performance.

Dataset Overview¶

The dataset consists of the marks secured by the students in various subjects, which accessible from Kaggle Student Performance in Exams. there are 1000 occurance and 8 columns:

  1. gender
  2. race / ethnicity
  3. parental level of education
  4. lunch
  5. test preparation course
  6. math score
  7. reading score
  8. writing score
